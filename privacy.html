Welcome to Pro Glass Tools. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how our website and online tools operate, what information may be collected, and how that information is used.

Important: Our Image to PDF Converter processes files directly inside your browser. Your images are not uploaded to our servers during the conversion process.

How Our Image to PDF Converter Works

The Image to PDF Converter available on this website uses browser-based processing technology. When you select images and create a PDF file, the conversion process takes place on your own device.

Because the conversion is performed locally in your browser, your files generally remain under your control and are not intentionally stored on our servers.
